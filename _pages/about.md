I am an Associate Professor of Astronomy at Indiana University Bloomington.

My research focuses on the **dynamical evolution of planetary systems**, with an emphasis on:

- exoplanet dynamics  
- planetary migration  
- secular interactions and instabilities  
- tidal evolution  
- the origin of Neptune-mass planets  

I combine **analytical theory and numerical simulations** to understand how planetary systems acquire their observed orbital architectures.

---

### Research

I am particularly interested in the dynamical pathways that shape planetary systems, including:

- migration and instability in resonant chains  
- high-eccentricity migration and tidal evolution  
- formation and evolution of **cold and hot Neptunes**  
- the role of planetesimal disks in breaking resonances  
- the production of free-floating planets
